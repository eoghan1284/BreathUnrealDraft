Breathwork is not just one breathing technique, there are a whole different range of techniques all with their own unique benefits.

What I offer?

Breakthrough Breathwork

Breakthrough breathwork is an incredibly powerful experience. One hour of guided breathwork which will put you into an altered state of consciousness. The thinking mind goes offline and the subconscious mind comes online. When our mind is constantly thinking we never get the opportunity to deal with the emotions we have buried deep down. We try to think ourselves out of these emotions, change the narrative in our mind, but the emotions are still stored away. During this breakthrough, we get to feel, express and release the emotions we are holding onto so that we don't have to carry them around with us anymore. People report having outer body experiences, psychedelic experiences and healing experiences all because of the power of the breath.

Breathing Retraining Programme

We all want to breathe a bit easier. However, the stressful world we live in speeds up our breathing which has negative impacts on all of our physical and mental wellbeing. The goal of this programme is to regain control over our day to day, functional breathing. You will learn to breathe low, breathe less and breathe slow. Learning to breathe correctly can:

  • Improve Sleep
  • Increase Energy
  • Reduce Stress
  • Enhance Athletic Performance

The programme consists of a series of simple breathwork techniques and breath holds that you can practice every day and log in your 'Breathing Retraining Programme' booklet which will be given to you should you sign up for the programme.

Breathwork Workshops

As mentioned before, there are so many different breathwork techniques. Some slow and relaxing some fast and energising. At breathwork workshops, I will teach you a whole range of different breathing exercises, go through how they can help you and guide you through them. As well as learning about breathwork, workshops are a great way to connect and interact with like minded people and share some stories. Even if you have never tried anything like this before they are a fun way to introduce yourself into the world of breathwork.
