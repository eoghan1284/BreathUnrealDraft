Welcome to Breathe Unreal

My name is Colm Galvin and I am a breathwork facilitator.
Breathwork changed my life, from learning how to change my daily breathing habits to reduce stress and anxiety, to having incredibly profound experiences to help shift my whole mindset doing breakthrough breathwork and so much more.
There are so many different types of breathwork for different situations in life and my goal is to help show people the power of the breath and how it can change their life.
